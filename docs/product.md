# TidyNest Product

## Vision

TidyNest helps people and households organize recurring home tasks, reduce mental load, and keep routines consistent.

## Problem Statement

Many homes rely on ad-hoc reminders and memory for chores. Tasks are missed, repeated inconsistently, or carried by one person. Existing tools are often too generic for household routines.

## Target Users

- Individuals managing personal home routines
- Households sharing chores and recurring responsibilities
- Busy users who want lightweight planning, not project-management complexity

## Core Outcomes

- Make routine tasks visible and easy to complete
- Reduce forgotten chores through clear scheduling and reminders
- Provide a simple sense of progress and shared accountability

## Product Principles

- Clarity over complexity
- Fast input and minimal friction
- Useful defaults, easy customization
- Mobile-friendly daily use

## Initial Scope (MVP)

- Task list with create, edit, complete, delete
- Due dates and recurrence basics
- Status filtering (all, due, done)
- Simple dashboard (today/this week)

## Out of Scope (MVP)

- Advanced automation rules
- Marketplace/integrations ecosystem
- Enterprise-level role management

## Success Signals

- Users complete tasks consistently week over week
- Time to add a task is under 30 seconds
- Weekly active usage remains stable after onboarding
